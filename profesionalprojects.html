VIRTUAL REALITY
I had the chance to work on multiple custom VR applications for different clients.
Like this small experience developed with Unity and C# for the Meta Quest. It allows attendants to the Olot Saints Museum in Catalonia to participate in the creation of one of their handmade religious images.
EDEBÉ WORLD
We made the web site for the school book publisher Edebé. We did it with Playcanvas, Javascript and HTML.
The web site invites you to discover their catalogue exploring little thematic dioramas. Different planets represent the stages of the schooling process and their islands, each of the different subjects.
