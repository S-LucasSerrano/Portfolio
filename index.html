L'APRENENT
As a professional developer, I've had the chance to work on multiple custom applications for different clients.
Like this VR experience that allows attendants to the Olot's Saints Museum in Catalonia to participate in the creation of one of their handmade religious images.
After visiting the museum and watching how they create the figures, you can put on the VR headset and make one yourself!
EDEBÉ WORLD
I was part of the team that made the web site EdebeWorld for the school book publisher Edebé. I programmed the 3D sections with Playcanvas, Javascript and HTML.
The web site invites you to discover their catalogue exploring little thematic dioramas. The different planets represent the stages of the schooling process and their islands, each of the different subjects.
